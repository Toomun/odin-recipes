Pasta with Peas and Sausage

Solve your dinner or lunch in an easy and dilecious way.

Enjoy a unique culinary experience with this Pasta with Peas and Sausage. Immerse yourself in a sea of exquisite flavors as fresh peas and artisan sausages come together in a harmonious dance of textures and aromas.

This dish will transport you directly to the beautiful Italian countryside in the height of the spring season. Ready to delight yourself with an explosion of flavor in every bite? Do not resist this unforgettable gastronomic experience!

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of pasta (it can be penne, fusilli, spaghetti or whatever you prefer).
  • 150 grams of peas (fresh or frozen).
  • 200 grams of sausages (they can be Italian sausages, chorizo or your favorite).
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped.
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced.
  • 1 can (400 grams) crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce.
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, salt and pepper to taste.
  • Parmesan or grated cheese for serving (optional).
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta: in a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta following package directions until al dente. Drain and reserve.
  2. Cook the sausages: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sausages and cook until golden brown on all sides and cooked through on the inside. Remove the sausages from the pan and cut them into slices or chunks. reserve them.
  3. cook the onion and garlic: in the same skillet where you cooked the sausages, add the chopped onion and garlic. Saut√© until the onion is transparent and the garlic releases its aroma.
  4. Add the peas and tomato sauce: add the peas to the pan and stir to combine with the onion and garlic. Then, pour in the tomato sauce and add the dried oregano. Stir everything together and let it cook over medium low heat for about 5 minutes so that the flavors blend.
  5. Mix the pasta and sausages: add the cooked pasta to the pan with the tomato sauce and peas. also add the sliced sausages. Stir well so that all the ingredients are mixed and heated evenly.
  6. Season and serve: taste the pasta and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, sprinkle grated or Parmesan cheese on top before serving. You can decorate with fresh basil leaves to give it a touch of color and aroma.
