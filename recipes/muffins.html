Chocolate chip muffins

These muffins are so delicious and easy to make that they will become a favorite at home.

These Chocolate Chip Muffins are the perfect combination of fluffiness and chocolate flavor. They are ideal for a sweet breakfast, a snack or even to share at a meeting with friends. Best of all, its preparation is so simple that anyone can do it, even if you have no cooking experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose wheat flour.
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar.
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • 3/4 cup of milk.
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil.
  • 1 large egg.
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract.
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips.

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 200Â°C and place paper liners in a muffin tin, or lightly grease the tin if you don't have liners.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Make sure all the dry ingredients are well integrated.
  3. In another smaller bowl, combine the milk, vegetable oil, egg, and vanilla essence. Beat the mixture until you get a homogeneous consistency.
  4. Pour the liquid ingredients into the dry ingredient pan and mix everything together with a spatula or wooden spoon. The key to fluffy muffins is not to over-mix; the dough should be slightly lumpy.
  5. Add the chocolate chips to the batter and mix lightly to distribute evenly.
  6. Fill each muffin pan cavity about 2/3 full with batter. This will allow the muffins plenty of room to rise while they bake.
  7. Bake the muffins in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
  8. Once done, remove the muffins from the oven and allow them to cool in the pan for a few minutes before removing them to a wire rack to cool completely.
